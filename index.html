About Us

KONIKZ Petshop is dedicated to providing top-quality pet products and services for all pet lovers. Founded with a passion for animals, we started as a small shop aiming to offer everything pets need in one place. Today, we continue to focus on quality, care, and customer satisfaction, ensuring your pets thrive with the best supplies and support.

Giving Back to the Community


As an organization of animal lovers, we are always looking at ways to support the local animal rescue community. We host regular weekend adoption days and have had the privilege of helping hundreds of dogs, cats & rabbits find their forever homes. If you are looking to add a new fur-ever member to your family, click here to find out more.

Fast and Convenient Delivery


Your time is important to us. That is why we offer door-to-door delivery across the Philippines, seven days a week.

Largest Pet Store in the Philippines


We carry over 12,000 products online and in our stores in Metro, Manila and outside countries.

Exclusive Brands


We understand the relationship you have with your pet, which is why as a rule we do not cut corners or disregard the need for high-quality products and risk their well-being. Our range of pet products includes a large variety of pet food, toys, healthcare, supplements, grooming and other accessories. With our selection of exclusive brands and products, we strive to fulfil all the requirements for both you and your pet whether by pet breed, size, age and more.
